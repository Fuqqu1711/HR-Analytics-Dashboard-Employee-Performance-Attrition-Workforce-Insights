# 👥 HR Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📊 Project Overview
This HR Analytics Dashboard is a comprehensive Power BI project designed to analyze workforce data and deliver actionable insights for HR and management teams. It focuses on employee attrition, engagement, performance, recruitment costs, and overall workforce distribution to support strategic decision-making.

The dashboard is structured across multiple views to reflect real-world HR reporting and stakeholder requirements.

---

## 🎯 Key Business Objectives
- Monitor employee attrition and retention trends
- Analyze workforce distribution across departments and locations
- Track employee performance, satisfaction, and engagement levels
- Evaluate recruitment costs and hiring sources
- Identify operational and performance improvement areas

---

## 📌 Key KPIs & Insights
- **Attrition Rate (%)**
- **Total, Active & Terminated Employees**
- **Employee Satisfaction & Engagement Scores (1–5)**
- **Average Pay Rate**
- **Recruitment Cost by Hiring Source**
- **Employee Tenure Distribution**
- **Performance Rating Distribution**
- **Department-wise Workforce Analysis**
- **Gender & State-wise Employee Distribution**

---

## 📊 Dashboard Sections
### 🏠 Workforce Overview
- Total employees, active employees, terminated employees
- Workforce distribution by department
- Gender and geographic distribution

### 📈 Employee Performance & Engagement
- Employee satisfaction and engagement gauges
- Performance rating distribution
- Operational error rate trends

### 🔄 Attrition & Retention Analysis
- Attrition rate with key drivers
- Employee tenure analysis
- Top reasons for employee attrition

### 💰 Recruitment & Cost Analysis
- Total recruitment cost
- Cost by hiring source
- Department-wise hiring insights
